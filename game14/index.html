• You can drag vertices
  • Click on two vertices in turn with pressed 'shift' to create an edge between them
  • Click on empty place to create a new vertice
  • Click on a vertice with pressed 'alt' to disable/enable it
  • Click on a vertice with pressed 'alt + shift' to delete it
  • Click on a vertice with pressed 'ctrl' to start extract a circle to create edges in it's area
